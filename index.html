About Me

I am a Doctoral Researcher at the University of Turku focusing on population genetics and the genetic effects of natural selection using ancient DNA and biobank-scale datasets.

My research explores how historical selection pressures — particularly infectious diseases — have shaped genetic diversity in the Finnish population, integrating ancient and modern genomic data.
